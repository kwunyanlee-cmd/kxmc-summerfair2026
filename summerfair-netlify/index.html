Why reprovision重建願景

Rooted in King's Cross: Rebuilding, Renewing, Transforming Our Church扎根君王十字：重建、更新、轉化我們的教會

King's Cross Methodist Church has served the local Camden community since the early 19th century, and is thriving today — with growing congregations, a student residential community and space for local groups. The ageing building now needs significant modernisation. All proceeds from the Summer Fair support this rebuilding project. 君王十字堂自十九世紀初已服侍 Camden 社區，至今仍然蓬勃 — 會眾不斷增長，並設有學生宿舍及社區團體聚會空間。年事漸高的建築現需大幅更新。夏日嘉年華的所有收益，將支持這項重建計劃。
